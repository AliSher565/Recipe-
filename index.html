CHICKEN SANDWITCH

A delicious mix of mayonnaise, chicken, pepper and some veggies spread on the bread all done at home in no time!.
This quick and easy dish is sure to be on your everyday breakfast menu. These types of sandwitches are loved by people belonging to any age group specially Childrens..

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup chicken boiled
  • 2 Tablespoon Mayonaise
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 2 bread pieces
  • 1 capsicum
  • 1 lettuce

Method

  1. Shred small pieces of boiled chicken in a bowl
  2. Add mayonaise and pepper to it
  3. Mix it thoroughly
  4. Take a slice of bread and spread the mixture on it
  5. Now chop capsicum and lettuce into small pieces
  6. Put them on the slice
  7. Put another slice on them and cut them in two
  8. Serve with Tomato ketchup or coriander chutney
